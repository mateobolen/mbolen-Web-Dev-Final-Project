Waterman Green Frolf Course

On University Of Vermont Campus, there is a little known frisbee golf course that runs on the Waterman Green and whose details and traditions have been passed down in the ultimate frisbee club for generations.

General course rules:

  • Each hole is par 3.
  • If your frisbee lands on concrete, the next throw must be upside down
  • If a bystander not part of the game picks up your frisbee and throws it, you must take it from where it lands.
